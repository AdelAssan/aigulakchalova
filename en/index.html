About Me

Leadership is not following the crowd,
but building the road

Aigul Akchalova is a deputy of the Zhambyl regional maslikhat, a successful entrepreneur and public figure. For over 20 years, she has contributed to regional development, job creation, and support of social initiatives.

Her approach combines business thinking with deep social responsibility. Each project is a contribution to the future of the Zhambyl region and Kazakhstan as a whole.
